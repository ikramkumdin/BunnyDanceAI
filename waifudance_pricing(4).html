Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Cancel your monthly subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your billing period. Annual plans are non-refundable after 7 days.
What's the difference between a subscription and credit packs?+
Subscriptions give you a fixed monthly quota of videos plus tier-exclusive features like priority queue, bulk export, and exclusive effects. Credit packs are one-time purchases — you buy credits once and spend them whenever you want, with no time limit. Credit packs are best for occasional users who don't need a monthly commitment.
Do credits expire?+
Credit pack credits never expire — they're tied to your account forever. Monthly subscription quotas, however, reset each billing cycle and do not roll over.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay via Stripe.
Do you offer refunds?+
We offer a 7-day refund window for new monthly subscribers. Credit packs are non-refundable as credits are consumed upon use. Annual plans are non-refundable after the 7-day window.
What are exclusive effects?+
Exclusive effects are released to higher tiers first. Standard users get 3 effects 30 days before general availability. Pro users get all exclusive effects immediately with no delay.
